------------------STACK------------------

1.Defining a stack.
---> stack<int> s;  //stack of integer type

2.Pushing into stack.
---> s.push(el);

3.Poping from stack.
---> s.pop();

4.Check top element of stack.
---> s.top();

5.Check stack is empty.
---> s.empty();

---> q.pop();

4.Check front element of queue.
---> q.front();

5.Check back element of queue.
---> q.back();

6.check size of queue.
